## 3. Lawful Bases for Processing

We process your personal data based on the following lawful bases under GDPR:

**Legal Processing Bases:**

- **Responding to inquiries:** Legitimate interest (6(1)(f)) or Contract (6(1)(b))
- **Providing legal services:** Contract (6(1)(b))
- **Marketing communications (with consent):** Consent (6(1)(a))
- **Website analytics:** Consent (6(1)(a)) or Legitimate interest (6(1)(f))
- **Legal obligations (e.g., AML, bar rules):** Legal obligation (6(1)(c))

For **special category data** (e.g., sensitive personal data in legal matters), we rely on GDPR Article 9(2)(f) (legal claims) or explicit consent.

## 8. Your Data Subject Rights (GDPR)

You have the following rights under GDPR:

**Your GDPR Rights:**

- **Access (Art. 15):** Request a copy of your personal data
- **Rectification (Art. 16):** Correct inaccurate or incomplete data  
- **Erasure (Art. 17):** Request deletion ("right to be forgotten")
- **Restriction (Art. 18):** Limit how we process your data
- **Data Portability (Art. 20):** Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
- **Object (Art. 21):** Object to processing based on legitimate interest
- **Withdraw Consent (Art. 7(3)):** Withdraw consent for marketing or analytics
- **Complain (Art. 77):** Lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority

**To exercise your rights:**  
Email [nader.bakri@cybergl.com](mailto:nader.bakri@cybergl.com) with your request. We will respond within **1 month** (may be extended by 2 months for complex requests).

**Note on legal services:**  
If you are a current or former client, we may be required to retain certain data under bar association rules, legal obligations, or to defend legal claims. We will explain any limitations on your rights in our response.

## 9. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

- **Encryption:** TLS/HTTPS for data in transit; AES-256 for data at rest (where applicable)
- **Access controls:** Role-based access, multi-factor authentication (MFA)
- **Secure infrastructure:** Cloud providers with ISO 27001, SOC 2 certifications
- **Regular audits:** Internal security reviews and vendor assessments

If we become aware of a personal data breach that poses a risk to your rights and freedoms, we will notify you and the relevant supervisory authority as required by GDPR.
